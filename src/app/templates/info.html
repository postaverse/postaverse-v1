-- Important --

Postaverse does not use your private information in any way.

Your password is only used so you can log in and change your password.

Passwords are encrypted and stored securely in a server-side database.

The single cookie is required for the site to work to make sure you are logged in and have a user id.

The above is required by GDPR for users to know and have the ability to change personal information.

In this case, the personal information is your password and soon email.

If you are under 13 years of age, please use this site with caution, as required by COPPA



How to use Postaverse

Go to Create Post to make a new post.

Tap on someone's name on a post to go to their profile.

Tap on a post's content to view the post's page.

You can like and comment from a post's page.
